Must-Visit Places in Kyoto

Kinkaku-ji

The Golden Pavilion, a Zen temple with stunning architecture and gardens.

Fushimi Inari Taisha

Famous for its thousands of red torii gates winding up the mountain.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Walk through towering bamboo stalks in a serene atmosphere.

Gion District

A historic area known for its traditional tea houses and geisha culture.
